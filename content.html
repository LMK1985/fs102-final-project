Why QuotStation

Once you have put in a request, sit back have a coffee and quotes will come in automatically.

Transparent & Safe

With reviews from other users and escrow services from us, your risks are kept low. Your time, money and efforts are protected from scams, inferior deliverables and subpar suppliers.

Good & Wide Variety

Your request is sent through to a robust list of relevant suppliers to get maximum exposure.

Free Buyer Account

Free buyer accounts offer all buyer features for you to create requests and get quotes quickly.

Pro Market Intelligence

Pro Accounts get access to critical data like product trends, winning bid prices and usage statistics.

Getting Quotes - Buying

Step 1

Step 1

Create a buyer account on QuotStation. It's free to use. Browse around for the products or services you are interested in after login.

Step 2

Step 2

Create a Request for Quotation for the product or service you wish to purchase. Fill in the relevant details and submit the request.

Step 3

Step 3

After some time, review the quotations you have received and choose one to accept. Make payment to QuotStation and you are all set to receive your products or services.

Giving Quotes - Selling

Step 1

Signup for a Business Seller or Professional Corporate account. Fill out your business details and the product or service categories offered by your business.

Step 2

You will start receiving requests for quotation relevant to your business offerings and you can view and bid for current requests in the account main page. For professional accounts, the Insights section can be accessed for critical data.

Step 3

After buyer confirmation on an accepted quotation is achieved, you can be rest assured to fulfill the order as payment has been put up by the buyer. Upon confirmation of completion, payment will be sent to your registered bank account.
